Products

From the UV to the Far-IR, we turn lasers into fully phase-corrected Dual Comb Spectrometers !

Our software has been tested and is deployed with lasers from Menhir, IPG, Menlo, NIST Sinclair's design and more.

Tested and works for laser repetition rates from 80 MHz to 1 GHz, with repetition rates difference between 20 Hz and 100 kHz.

Software - the PUG

This is the only product on the market that flexibly allows using any combination of referencing signals (or none !) and performs real-time fast as well as self correction on your dual comb interferograms.
